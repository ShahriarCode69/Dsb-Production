Frequently Asked Questions

Who is your service for?

Our services cater to YouTubers, content creators, brands, and anyone looking to elevate their visual content.

How do I get started?

Simply fill out our contact form or click the

button to schedule a consultation.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payments through PayPal, Stripe, Payoneer, Wise and bank transfers.

Do you require a deposit?

Yes that depends, a 20-30% deposit is required upfront, with the balance due upon project completion. But we can move to a marketplace if you want to make your payment more secure.

Can I request revisions?

Absolutely! We include upto 10 free revisions in our package to ensure you're happy with the final product.

What tools/software do you use?

We use industry-standard tools like Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, and DaVinci Resolve.

What is your refund policy?

Refunds are available only for projects not yet started. Deposits are non-refundable after work begins. Do you retain rights to the edited videos? No, once the final payment is made, all rights to the video belong to you.
