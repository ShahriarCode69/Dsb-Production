How We Work

Step into the heart of DSB Studio's expertise, where creativity meets precision. Explore our meticulous video editing process to bring your vision to life with unmatched quality and efficiency.

Consultation & Onboarding

When a client reaches out to us, we start with an initial consultation—whether through email, video call, or chat.

Proposal and Quote

We offer a comprehensive proposal detailing project scope, turnaround time, and budget details, ensuring client comprehension and allowing for agreement before proceeding to the next step.

Collecting Assets & Footage

Once the project is confirmed, the client provides the necessary raw footage, audio files, voiceovers, scripts, or any other media through Google Drive, Dropbox, or other preferred file-sharing platforms.

Editing The Content

After collecting footages and assets, we cut the unnessecery parts of the videos and add all types of graphics, sfx, texts to make the video look good

Revisions and Feedback Loop

Once the polished version is ready, we share it through tools like Frame.io, Google Drive, or the client's preferred method for feedback. We encourage clients to provide specific feedback so we can make any necessary adjustments.

Final Delivery and Handover

After implementing feedback, we prepare the final video for delivery. Rendering the video in the required resolution and format. Delivering all relevant files, including project files and assets, if requested.
